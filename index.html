BSCS Educational Tour

A four-day company visit, activities, lectures, and workshops

Day 1

Visited MOA for some window shopping and sightseeing, enjoying the lively atmosphere and storefront displays.

Day 2

Visiting the PAGASA Weather Station was an eye-opening experience. I got to see firsthand how meteorologists monitor weather patterns and use advanced instruments to track typhoons, rainfall, and temperature.

Day 3

Our visit to the QBA Innovation Hub was both inspiring and exciting. The space was filled with modern technology, creative projects, and passionate innovators. We were introduced to various startup ideas and tech-driven solutions that are shaping the future.

Day 4

Exploring the National Museum of the Philippines was a meaningful and enriching experience. I was amazed by the wide collection of artifacts, artworks, and historical pieces that reflect our country's rich culture and heritage.
